Hello! I am a Ph.D. student in Interdisciplinary Studies in Human Development at the University of Pennsylvania Graduate School of Education.

My research examines how risk and protective factors, along with the intersection of home, school, and neighborhood environments, shape child and adolescent development in global contexts. I aim to use research findings to create contextually relevant intervention programs, inform policies, and form reliable resources that support children, adolescents, and their families.

My work has been published in top journals in the field, including Developmental Psychology, American Journal of Psychiatry, and International Journal of Early Childhood.
